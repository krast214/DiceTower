Silent Karaoke - Quick Rules

Objective: Guess the song or phrase your friends are "singing"—without a sound! Each round, one player gets a secret prompt and must act it out using only gestures and lip-syncing. The rest try to guess as quickly as possible. No humming, no mouthing words, just pure silent performance!

How to Play:

  1. Join a room and wait for everyone to arrive.
  2. Host starts the game.
  3. Each round, a player is chosen as the "Performer" and receives a secret prompt.
  4. The Performer acts out the prompt silently. Others type guesses in chat.
  5. First correct guess wins the round!
  6. Rotate and repeat. Most points at the end wins.

Tip: Be expressive! The more dramatic, the better.
