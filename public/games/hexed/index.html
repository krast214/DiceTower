Hexed! - Quick Rules

Objective: Make the best connections between your hex tiles and guess the links made by others.

How to Play:

  1. Each player receives a board of hex tiles with random words.
  2. Players write clues to connect the words on their board.
  3. Other players try to guess the original connections.
